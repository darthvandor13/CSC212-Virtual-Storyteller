Mapping[T_Attr, Callable[[], T_Attr]] anyio._core._typedattr.TypedAttributeProvider.extra_attributes (   self) 
A mapping of the extra attributes to callables that return the corresponding
values.

If the provider wraps another provider, the attributes from that wrapper should
also be included in the returned mapping (but the wrapper may override the
callables from the wrapped instance).

Reimplemented in anyio.streams.text.TextStream, anyio.streams.text.TextSendStream, anyio.streams.text.TextReceiveStream, anyio.streams.stapled.MultiListener, anyio.abc._sockets._SocketProvider, anyio.streams.text.TextStream, anyio.streams.text.TextSendStream, anyio.streams.text.TextReceiveStream, anyio.streams.stapled.MultiListener, and anyio.abc._sockets._SocketProvider.
